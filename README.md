# 🍽️ FoodSpire - Smart Food Management Platform

FoodSpire is a full-stack web application designed to reduce food waste and create a smart food ecosystem. It connects restaurants, users, and donors by providing restaurant booking, leftover food deals, food donations, homemade food services, and AI-based food assistance.

The platform focuses on sustainability by helping redistribute surplus food and making food services more accessible.


## 🚀 Features

### 🍴 Restaurant Management
- View available restaurants
- Explore restaurant details
- Restaurant booking system
- Customer reviews and ratings

### 🌙 Night Deals (Leftover Food)
- Restaurants can provide discounted leftover food
- Users can view available night deals
- Helps reduce food wastage

### 🎁 Food Donation System
- Donate surplus food
- Supports:
  - Restaurant food donations
  - Individual donations
  - Grocery donations
  - Meal donations
- Donation tracking system

### 🏠 Homemade Food
- Explore homemade food services
- Supports local food providers

### 🔔 Real-Time Notifications
- Firebase Cloud Messaging integration
- Users receive food updates and deal notifications

### 🤖 FoodSpire AI Assistant
- AI-powered food chatbot
- Food suggestions
- Nutrition guidance
- Recipe assistance
